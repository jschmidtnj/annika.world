biography: >-
  Annika is a writer-director, musicmaker, and multimedia artist based in New
  York. 


  Annika’s filmmaking practice seeks to tell bold and subversive stories which propagate multidimensional representations—transforming minds and shifting cultural paradigms through film and television. 


  Annika’s academic concentration specializes in the intersection of filmmaking, psychology, philosophy, multimedia art, and writing, which ultimately guides them to produce artworks which probe the mind.


  As a multidisciplinary artist, Annika’s approach to storytelling combines creative exploration and adaptability with close attention to the generic and formal strengths of each artistic mode. Annika’s artistic practices outside of filmmaking include tattooing, poetry and prose, comics, music production, DJing, fine art, and textile design.
aboutArt: >-
  Annika’s filmmaking examines mental health and wellbeing in contemporary
  times, personal and social identity in a digitized world, and constructions of
  reality. Annika often engages themes of queer love, migration, ambient
  dystopia, bodies, technology and decay, dream realms, cyborgism, cosmology,
  and tender alien nature. 


  Annika utilizes a deeply layered approach to storytelling, intensifying visual and musical narratives through a process of fragmentation and collage, in order to evoke variable states of consciousness and subjectivities in perception. The result is a bold and energized body of work inviting dissection.
